How Owshen works

Deposit

Start by depositing your funds to you Owshen address through an ordinary wallet!

Send

You can now start sending notes to other Owshen users through their Owshen addresses!

Withdraw

Once you want your funds back to your ordinary wallet, initiate a withdrawal!

Dive into the Owshen!

Worried of getting your source address flagged by directly depositing your funds into the Owshen's smart-contract? We have a solution!

How much DIVE do I get?

Depends on the market! Every 6 hours, a total of 50 DIVEs can be minted. The DIVEs are distributed between minters according to the amount they have burnt for that specific epoch!
